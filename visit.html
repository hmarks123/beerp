GETTING HERE

PARKING INFO

Valet and self-parking are available through the Paris Las Vegas hotel garage.

VALET PARKING

Available 24/7 at the Paris Las Vegas main entrance.

  • Fast and convenient drop-off
  • Direct elevator access to Beer Park
  • Standard valet rates apply
  • Complimentary for hotel guests

SELF-PARKING

Multi-level parking garage with easy access.

  • Enter from Flamingo Road or Las Vegas Blvd
  • Over 3,000 spaces available
  • Well-lit and secure facility
  • Standard parking rates apply

RIDESHARE

Uber and Lyft drop-off at Paris Las Vegas main entrance.

  • Designated rideshare pickup zone
  • Quick access to rooftop
  • No parking fees
  • Recommended for late nights

PUBLIC TRANSIT

Accessible via Las Vegas public transportation.

  • Deuce bus stops directly in front
  • Las Vegas Monorail nearby
  • SDX express bus service available
  • Affordable and convenient option
